About Dot Meditation

Dot Meditation is a simple, distraction-free practice where you focus on a single dot to calm your mind, improve concentration, and build mindfulness.

How It Works

  • Choose your meditation duration and dot size.
  • Enter full-screen mode for a clean, distraction-free experience.
  • Focus on the dot to improve awareness and relaxation.

Benefits

  • Reduces stress and anxiety
  • Improves focus and mental clarity
  • Supports daily mindfulness habits
